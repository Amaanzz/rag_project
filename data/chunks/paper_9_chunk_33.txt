D.5 Results of trained IR mdels Experimental results on TOOLRET reveal that even IR models with strong performance on conventional IR benchmarks such as MTEB and BEIR struggle significantly in tool retrieval tasks. A key factor contributing to this performance degradation is the lack of a large-scale training dataset specifically tailored for tool retrieval. To address this gap, we introduce TOOLRET-train, a diverse training dataset comprising more than 200k tool retrieval tasks.
