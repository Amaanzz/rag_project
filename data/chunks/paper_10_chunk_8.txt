CONCLUSION In this paper, we presented MusT-RAG, a retrieval- augmented framework that enhances text-only Music Question Answering (MQA) by adapting general-purpose LLMs to the music domain. By retrieving relevant pas- sages from a music-specific database and incorporating them into the generation context, MusT-RAG effectively mitigates the factuality limitations commonly observed in LLMs. As a result, our method achieves substantial im- provements over GPT-4o [35], particularly in factual ac- curacy.
