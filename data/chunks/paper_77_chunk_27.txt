The probability of making no Type I error in all |Λ| tests is (1 −δ |Λ|)|Λ|. The probability of making at least one Type I error (i.e., FWER) is the complement of making no Type I errors, which is 1 −(1 −δ |Λ|)|Λ| ≤δ.
