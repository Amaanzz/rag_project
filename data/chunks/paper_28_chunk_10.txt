In the development of Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) systems, selecting the right model, vector database, and cloud provider is critical to ensuring optimal performance and alignment with business objectives. The landscape of available technologies is vast, with each offering unique features, scalability options, and integration capabilities. For instance, different LLMs vary in their context windows, multimodal capabilities, and accessibility, inﬂuencing their suitability for speciﬁc use cases. Similarly, the choice of vector databases, directly impacts the system’s ability to efﬁciently manage and search through large datasets. Furthermore, the cloud provider must be carefully chosen to support the desired scalability, security, and compliance needs. Each of these decisions must be tailored to the speciﬁc requirements of the business case, as the right combination can signiﬁcantly enhance the effectiveness of the RAG system while ensuring it meets the operational demands and strategic goals of the organization.
