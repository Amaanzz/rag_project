(2023b) utilize online dictionaries and biencoder architectures for disambiguation and a matching module that selects the most appropriate modality to guide final retrieval. Their approach significantly differs from ours, both in the disam- biguation and the ranking part. Patil et al. (2023) rely on an ensemble approach involving fine-tuning CLIP and extracting a joint image-text representa- tion.
