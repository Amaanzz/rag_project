RelBench [57] reformulates relational databases as heterogeneous graphs for representation learning with GNNs. 5 Retrieval-And-Structuring The Retrieval-And-Structuring (RAS) paradigm unifies three com- plementary approaches to knowledge processing: information re- trieval, structured knowledge representation through Knowledge Graphs (KGs), and Large Language Models (LLMs). This integrated framework enhances LLMs’ ability to perform knowledge-intensive tasks, enables more effective knowledge discovery within struc- tured knowledge, and creates synergistic benefits across all three components.
