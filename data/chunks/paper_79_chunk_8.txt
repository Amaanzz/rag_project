In a MDP, the primary challenge lies in determining an optimal policy that guides an agent to select actions at states, with the aim of maximizing cumulative rewards. Given that the states within our MDPs are continuous, we em- ploy Deep Q-Networks (DQN) with replay mem- ory (Mnih et al., 2013) to learn the policy, denoted as ѕАќЄ(a(S)|s(S)) for Agent-S (resp. ѕАѕХ(a(R)|s(R)) for Agent-R). The policy samples an action a(S) (resp. a(R)) at a given state s(S) (resp. s(R)) via DQN, with parameters denoted by ќЄ (resp. ѕХ). Combining Agent-S and Agent-R.
