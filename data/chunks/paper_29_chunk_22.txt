As shown in Figure 9, our analysis of chunk candidate counts on WikiPas- sageQA demonstrates that CARROT achieves high response quality while requiring fewer iterations and thus fewer external model calls through its parallel expansion algorithm and pol- icy tree search mechanism. Across different chunk candidate counts, several methods require more external model calls to select their optimal retrieval output, whereas CARROT shows only a slight increase in external model call counts while maintaining them within an acceptable range and achieving response quality superior to baselines.
