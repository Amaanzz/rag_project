CONCLUSION In this paper, we present ITRG, which is an iterative retrieval- generation synergy framework, containing two important steps: generation-augmented retrieval and retrieval-augmented generation. They form a closed loop, and can improve each other via multiple iterations. We propose a simple and effective generation-augmented retrieval strategy and two retrieval-augmented generation strategies. Empirical re- sults show our approach significantly exceeds several strong baselines, including GPT 3.5, on four open domain ques- tion answering datasets, which indicates that our method can significantly improve the reasoning ability of large language models. 6.
