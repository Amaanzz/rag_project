However, these two models are over-sensitive. Specifically, Claude- 3 and Gemini classify 85.1% and 44.8% of the ChatGPT’s responses as unsafe, among which only 7.7% are unsafe according to human annotation. Therefore, adopting other famous LLMs as evalua- tors can lead to negative effects.
