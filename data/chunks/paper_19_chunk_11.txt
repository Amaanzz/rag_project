Therefore, we regard ChatGLM3, GPT-3.5-turbo, GPT4, Vicuna13b and Vicuna33b as LLMs demonstrating high coherence in logical consistency, as well as high consistency in the split-half reliability test, which indicates that they respond to the personality questionnaires like how humans would. Hence, their responses are deemed sufficiently reliable to be used for further personality analysis. This rigorous methodological approach provides a solid foundation for our exploration into the potential of LLMs to simulate human personality traits. C.
