B.5 Task format The final benchmark, TOOLRET, integrates the above datasets and reformats all test cases into a unified format, similar to conventional IR benchmarks such as BEIR and MTEB, to evaluate IR models in tool retrieval tasks. Each reformatted task consists of: an input query, an instruction, and the corresponding target tools (e.g., labels). Each tool is assigned a unique identifier and is paired with detailed documentation describing its functionality.
