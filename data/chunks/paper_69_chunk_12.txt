Ablation Study We conduct ablation study to explore the effects of the following factors. (1) Additional Training on Wikipedia. We study whether our improvement is from helping the model to achieve information refinement, or simply because of additional training on Wikipedia. To this end, we train LLaMA-2 on Wikipedia with standard language modeling objective, by setting the same hyperparameters as our INFO-RAG. The results in Table 5 show that this baseline leads to no improvement over the backbone LLaMA-2, con- firming the effectiveness of our training method rather than additional training on Wikipedia.
