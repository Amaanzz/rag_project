C Large-scaling training dataset: TOOLRET-train We extend the data collection process from TOOLRET to incorporate the training sets of three mainstream tool-use datasets: ToolACE (Liu et al., 2024a), ToolBench (Qin et al., 2023), and APIGen (Liu et al., 2024b). These datasets are selected for their diversity in task types, tool categories, and query complexity, ensuring a comprehensive representation of real-world tool-use scenarios. After collecting and preprocessing the data, we ultimately collect over 200k training instances. Each instance consists of a query and a corresponding set of target tools. Next, we further pair each query with an instruction using our target-aware strategy (§ 3.3). This strategy generates instructions that explicitly guide the retrieval process by incorporating task-specific context and tool functionality descriptions.
