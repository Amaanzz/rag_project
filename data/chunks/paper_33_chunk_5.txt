Unlike existing literature, which often utilizes LLMs on specific types of notes, our method harnesses RAG and MapReduce to effectively analyze the complete patient documentation. Our experiments demonstrated the superiority of this method over SME rule-based models in diagnosing PH. Efficient LLM-based phenotype models offer scalability and improvement in identifying specific diseases in real-world EHRs, reducing the manual workload for SMEs and the need for ad-hoc machine learning models while enabling comprehensive patient record analysis. This advancement promises to enhance systems utilizing EHRs for purposes such as clinical decision support, care gap detection / population health management, clinical trial matching, and cohort generation.
