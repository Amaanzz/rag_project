Discussions of limitations and future work Limitations One potential challenge of applying C-RAG practically could be the collection of calibration data. In practice, the user input texts are sampled from a time-series data distribution. Therefore, accessing in-distribution calibration samples requires collecting real-time query samples, which could pose the challenge of computational resources and system latency.
