Evaluation metrics The core of this benchmark is to evaluate whether LLMs can utilize the provided external documents to acquire knowl- edge and generate reasonable answers. We evaluate the re- sponses of LLMs in order to measure above-mentioned four abilities of them. Accuracy is used to measure noise robustness and infor- mation integration. We employ an exact matching approach where if the generated text contains an exact match to the answer, it is considered as a correct answer. Rejection rate is used to measure negative rejection. When only noisy documents are provided, LLMs should output the specific content – “I can not answer the question because of the insufficient information in documents.” (We use instructions to inform the model.). If the model gener- ates this content, it indicates a successful rejection. Error detection rate measures whether the model can detect the factual errors in the documents for counterfactual robustness.
