Enhancing inter-agent communication and collaboration is a key area of focus. By enabling agents to share intermediate insights or partial results, the system could better handle multi-faceted and complex queries, ensuring a seamless flow of information across agents. Another promising avenue lies in incorporating adaptive learning mechanisms.
