CONCLUSIONS Our study proposes a feasible framework for tender document generation, utilizing retrieval-augmented techniques and LLMs. The document generation process is mainly completed in two steps: selecting a template and then modifying it. During this process, multiple LLM-based agents interact with the user, verifying the necessary information for creating the tender document and generating content based on user requirements. In future work, we will focus on expanding the tender document corpus and exploring methods for handling complex table tasks.
