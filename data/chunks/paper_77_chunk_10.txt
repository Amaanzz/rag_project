C-RAG under distribution shifts Here, we present a valid distribution-drift conformal genera- tion risk and prove the benefit of RAG compared to vanilla LLM under test-time distribution shifts. 6.1. Analysis setup Conformal risk guarantees often assume that calibration and testing samples come from the same distribution (Bates et al., 2021; Angelopoulos et al., 2022; 2021). Next, build- ing on Sec. 5.1, we extend these guarantees to distribution shifts between calibration and testing. 6.2.
