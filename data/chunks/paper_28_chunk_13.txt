The success of any AI application, especially those leveraging large language models, is closely tied to the infrastruc- ture and services provided by major cloud providers. Studies [36] show that cloud giants like Amazon, Microsoft, and Google offer a comprehensive suite of AI and infrastructure services, including compute power, application de- velopment, security and compliance, and industry-speciﬁc solutions, underscoring the integral role of these cloud platforms in supporting AI development. These services are integral to the development, deployment, and scaling of AI applications. Choosing the right cloud vendor is a critical decision that must be made early in the project. This decision impacts not only the development and deployment phases but also the long-term sustainability and scalability of the AI application.
